What is Manimaran Studios 8?
Manimaran Studios 8 (OPC) Private Limited is a Chennai-based company focused on developing innovative strategic board games.
What is Thiral?
Thiral is a competitive strategic board game designed to test tactical thinking and battlefield decision-making skills.
